    Urban Seismic Risk Assessment
    1. Edit seismic source: input characteristics of seismic rupture generating ground motion at the target sites (Lorca and Puerto Lumbreras). Use Automatic or Manual menu:

      • Automatic menu: Find a source among the Available Faults of the list. Select it on the map and click Generate Max Magnitude, to estimate the seismic risk due to the maximum magnitude associated to this fault.
      • Manual menu: Click on Start to Edit. Introduce the source parameters. Use the Generate This Earthquake button below, to estimate the seismic risk due to the newly defined earthquake.
    2. Select the probability level associated to the seismic scenario:

      • High probability: median ground motion value and modal damage grade.
      • Low probability (high impact): median-plus-one-sigma ground motion value and modal damage grade.
      • Very low probability (very high impact): median-plus-one-sigma ground motion value and damage grade corresponding to percentile 85.
