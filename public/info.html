    Information

    This project develops a seismic-scenario simulator with two separate access profiles: one for expert users, who may run calculations of building damage and debris production at building level; and another for general users, who may view the expected damage aggregated by census sections only.

    It focuses on the municipalities of Lorca and Puerto Lumbreras, both with the same capabilities. Registered users can also compute points along road segments with different levels of obstruction caused by falling debris that restrict or block vehicle circulation. The coordinates of these points can be exported for the input into the emergency route-calculation system

    The first version of this project was developed in the frame of the MERISUR project, (ref. CGL2013-40492-R), National R+D+I Program for Societal Challenges 2013, Ministry of Economy and Competitiveness of Spain.

    This new version has been developed as part of the Twin-ER: Earthquake Risk Pilot Digital Twin project (PID2023-149468NB-I00). The Twin-ER project corresponds to the call for Knowledge Generation Projects 2023 of the National Plan for Scientific, Technical and Innovation Research 2021-2023 of Spain, with funding from the Ministry of Science, Innovation and Universities -State Agency for Research- and the European Regional Development Fund (MCIU/AEI/10.13039/501100011033/FEDER, UE).
